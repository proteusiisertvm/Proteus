RHETOR 4.0

Proteus, The Biology Club of IISER Thiruvananthapuram brings to you, our first ever online Symposium RHETOR 4.0! Rhetor started as a mini-symposium with guest speakers worldwide, indulging our audience in various exciting developments in the scientific arena. This time we are thrilled to conduct Rhetor with full fervor and at a larger scale with merchandise, events and talks!
