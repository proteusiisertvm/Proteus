Welcome address

Dr. N Sadananda Singh provides the welcome address to the science community at IISER Thriuvananthapuram and beyond about the nascent club from the school of biology, Proteus. Come join us in our exploration of 'life'

Video Transcript

Dear students and my dear faculty colleagues of the school of Biology, for some time now, the school of Biology wanted to have a Biology club. Today I'm going to introduce our biology club named Proteus. The club is comprised of 11 members for the time being, including Biology major students, BS-MS students, IPh.D., and Ph.D. students of the SoB. The club has planned many exciting and interesting activities weekly, monthly and fortnightly activities. These activities will be informed to you by the club very soon. This club will be all about discussing, learning, and promoting Sciences, especially biological sciences. We have discussed to make the activities and contributors to these activities to be age and valiant, and gender-balanced. I request your full support to make this a successful and interesting place to be. Thank you all very much.

In this digital age, I am sure the space such as this will allow budding as well as seasoned professionals to engage mutually and indulge in constructive enrichment. I wish ‘Proteus’ all the best in serving as a source of veritable information and state-of-the-art knowledge!

Prof. S. Murty Srinivasula

Professor | IISER Thriuvananthapuram

While the COVID-19 pandemic has forced the students indoors, away from campus, our students used the time to envisage this biology club: Proteus. I believe that this club has the potential to nurture scientific curiosity and reasoning among young brains, who possess a genuine enthusiasm to learn. The club opens opportunities, outside the classroom, from learning directly from scientists involved in discoveries to engaging in real-world science research and education. The club will also help in nurturing long-term academic relationships between club members and practising scientists in India and abroad. I wish the club all the best in their endeavours in making school a more fulfilling experience, and in inculcating a scientific temperament in the wider community.

Dr. Poonam Thakur

Assistant Professor | IISER Thriuvananthapuram

Proteus has been doing a great job in showcasing the wide range of research taking place at the School of Biology. This platform has bought together- the dynamism of budding scientists and the perspective of seasoned researchers. I congratulate the group and look forward to more successful events.
